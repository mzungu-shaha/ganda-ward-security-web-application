## 1. Project Overview

### 1.1 Purpose

The Ganda Ward Security Information System (GWSIS) is a comprehensive web-based platform designed to modernize crime incident tracking and management in Ganda Ward, Kilifi County, Kenya. The system enables law enforcement, village elders, and administrators to effectively monitor, report, and analyze crime incidents across all eight villages in the ward.

### 1.2 Problem Statement

**Current Challenges:**
- Manual paper-based incident reporting
- Difficulty tracking incidents across 8 villages
- Limited visibility into crime trends and patterns
- Slow information sharing between stakeholders
- Inefficient report generation for authorities

**Solution:**
Centralized digital platform with real-time incident tracking, analytics, and reporting capabilities.

### 4.1 Technology

| Component | Technology |
|-----------|------------|
| Platform | Web-based (Next.js 16) |
| Database | SQLite (Local) |
| Authentication | JWT + Role-Based |
