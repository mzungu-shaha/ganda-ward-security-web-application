## Demo Script

### Opening (1 minute)

> "Good morning/afternoon. Thank you for your time. Today I'll demonstrate the Ganda Ward Security Information System - a new digital platform for tracking and managing crime incidents across our ward."

### Scene 1: Login (2 minutes)

**Say**: "Let's start by accessing the system."

**Actions**:
1. Navigate to login page
2. Enter credentials: `admin` / `admin123`
3. Click Login

**Say**: "The system uses secure authentication with role-based access. Different users see different features based on their role."

---

### Scene 2: Dashboard Overview (4 minutes)

**Say**: "This is the main dashboard - your command center for security operations."

**Actions**:
1. Point out statistics cards:
   - Total Incidents: 20
   - Open Cases: 12
   - Resolved Cases: 8
   - Arrests Made: 3

2. Show charts:
   - "This bar chart shows incidents by village - helping us identify high-risk areas"
   - "The pie chart displays crime type distribution"
   - "The line chart tracks monthly trends"
   - "The doughnut chart shows case status"

3. Point to recent incidents list

**Say**: "All key information at a glance - updated in real-time."

---

### Scene 3: Incident Management (4 minutes)

**Say**: "Now let's look at incident management - the core of the system."

**Actions**:
1. Click "Incidents" in sidebar
2. Show filter options (village, crime type, status, date)
3. Click "+ New Incident"
4. Fill in sample incident:
   - Village: Ganda
   - Crime Type: Theft
   - Description: "Reported theft at market"
   - Location: "Central Market"
   - Suspect Status: Unknown
5. Click Submit

**Say**: "Anyone can report incidents - police officers, village elders, even community members in the future."

---

### Scene 4: Viewing Incident Details (2 minutes)

**Actions**:
1. Click on an existing incident
2. Show full details

**Say**: "Every incident has complete details - location, evidence, timeline, assigned officer, and status."

---

### Scene 5: Crime Map (3 minutes)

**Say**: "One of our most powerful features - the interactive crime map."

**Actions**:
1. Click "Map" in sidebar
2. Point to markers
3. Click a marker to show popup
4. Demonstrate zoom and pan

**Say**: "Visual representation helps identify crime hotspots and allocate resources effectively."

---

### Scene 6: Reports (3 minutes)

**Say**: "Generating reports is simple and fast."

**Actions**:
1. Click "Reports"
2. Select "PDF Export"
3. Select "Monthly Report"
4. Choose month
5. Click "Generate PDF"
6. Show download

**Say**: "Reports can be generated instantly - saving hours of manual work."

---

### Scene 7: User Management (1 minute, Admin only)

**Say**: "Administrators can manage all system users."

**Actions**:
1. Show Users page
2. Demonstrate creating a new user

**Say**: "Complete control over who accesses the system and what they can do."

---

### Closing (1 minute)

**Say**: "This is GWSIS - modern efficient, and designed, for our ward's needs. Questions?"

# Part 2: Training Plan

## Training Overview

### Training Objectives

By the end of training, participants will be able to:
- Log in and navigate the system
- Create, view, and manage incident reports
- Use the dashboard for decision-making
- Generate reports for stakeholders
- Apply role-specific permissions appropriately

---

## Training Schedule

### Phase 1: Administrator Training (2 hours)

| Session | Duration | Content |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Session 1 | 30 min | System overview, login, navigation |
| Session 2 | 45 min | User management, role configuration |
| Session 3 | 30 min | Report generation, exports |
| Session 4 | 15 min | Q&A, practice |

**Participants**: 1-2 administrators

### Phase 2: Police Officer Training (2 hours)

| Session | Duration | Content |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Session 1 | 30 min | System overview, login, navigation |
| Session 2 | 45 min | Incident management (CRUD) |
| Session 3 | 30 min | Dashboard, map, reports |
| Session 4 | 15 min | Q&A, practice |

**Participants**: All police officers (3-5)

### Phase 3: Village Elder Training (1.5 hours)

| Session | Duration | Content |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Session 1 | 30 min | System overview, login, navigation |
| Session 2 | 45 min | Viewing incidents, reporting new incidents |
| Session 3 | 15 min | Q&A, practice |

**Participants**: All village elders (8)

### Phase 4: Viewer Training (1 hour)

| Session | Duration | Content |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Session 1 | 30 min | System overview, login, navigation |
| Session 2 | 20 min | Viewing dashboard and reports |
| Session 3 | 10 min | Q&A |

**Participants**: County officials, viewers
